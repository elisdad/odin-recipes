Wings

Description

This is a how to grill the wings.

This is another paragraph on how to make the wings.

Ingredients

  • chicken wings
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Texas Pete wing sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  2. Salt and pepper wings
  3. Place wings on the grill
  4. Grill for 20 minutes, flipping frequently
  5. Remove wings from grill
  6. Tops wings in sauce
