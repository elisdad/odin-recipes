Burgers

Description

This is a how to grill the burger.

This is another paragraph on how to make the burger.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger Meat
  • Bun
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  2. Form burger patties
  3. Place patties on the grill
  4. Grill for 4 minutes
  5. Flip patties
  6. Add cheese
  7. Grill for 4 more minutes
  8. Remove patties from grill
  9. Place on bottom bun
  10. Add lettuce
  11. Add tomoto
  12. Add pickles
  13. Add mustard
  14. Add top bun
