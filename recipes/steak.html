Description

This is a how to grill the steak.

This is another paragraph on how to make the steak.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • salt
  • pepper
  • A1 steak sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to 400 degrees
  2. Salt and pepper steak
  3. Place steak on the grill
  4. Grill for 4 minutes
  5. Flip steak
  6. Grill for 4 more minutes for medium steak
  7. Remove steak from grill
  8. Top with A1 sauce
